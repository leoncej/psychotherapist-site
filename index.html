Welcome

If you’re seeking therapy then chances are you’re in a situation which is causing you some difficulty. Therapy and counselling can help you to:

Manage difficult emotions

Gain greater control of your life

Find more meaning and purpose in your life

Work through difficult life situations

Be better able to have meaningful relationships

Experience greater confidence

I am a professionally trained and qualified, UKCP registered psychotherapist and counsellor. I practice from three Central London locations, in Gower Street, Fitzrovia (WC1), Holborn / Chancery Lane (WC1), and Little Venice, Paddington (W2).

I have experience working with:

Relationships: romantic, workplace etc

Body image and eating issues, including weight loss and compulsive eating

Stress, workplace and career issues

Childhood trauma and abuse

Addictions

Grief
